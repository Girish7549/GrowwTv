Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Overview

At GrowwTv, we strive to provide the best streaming experience possible. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be provided and the process for requesting them.

2. Free Trial Period

We offer a 7-day free trial for new subscribers. During this period, you can cancel your subscription at any time without being charged. If you cancel before the trial period ends, you will not be billed.

3. Subscription Refunds

3.1 General Policy

Subscription fees are generally non-refundable. However, we may provide refunds in certain circumstances at our sole discretion.

3.2 Eligible Refund Scenarios

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • Technical issues that prevent you from accessing the service for an extended period
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Unauthorized charges on your account
  • Service outages lasting more than 24 hours
  • Cancellation within 48 hours of initial subscription (excluding free trial users)

3.3 Non-Eligible Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after the free trial period
  • Failure to cancel before automatic renewal
  • Inability to access content due to geographic restrictions
  • Dissatisfaction with available content
  • Account suspension due to violation of Terms of Service

4. Partial Refunds

In cases where a refund is approved, we typically provide a full refund for the current billing period. Partial refunds for unused portions of a subscription period are not generally provided, except in cases of extended service outages or technical issues.

5. How to Request a Refund

5.1 Contact Information

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team:

  • Email: refunds@GrowwTv.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website

5.2 Required Information

When requesting a refund, please provide:

  • Your account email address
  • Order or transaction ID
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Any relevant supporting documentation

6. Refund Processing

6.1 Review Process

All refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We aim to respond to refund requests within 3-5 business days.

6.2 Processing Time

If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-10 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the subscription.

6.3 Payment Method Considerations

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-14 business days

7. Gift Subscriptions

Gift subscriptions are generally non-refundable once activated by the recipient. However, unused gift subscriptions may be eligible for refund within 30 days of purchase.

8. Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions may be eligible for prorated refunds in exceptional circumstances, such as extended service outages or technical issues that prevent service access for significant periods.

9. Third-Party Purchases

If you purchased your GrowwTv subscription through a third-party platform (such as Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or Amazon), refund requests must be made directly through that platform according to their refund policies.

10. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or credit card company without first contacting us, your account may be suspended. We encourage you to contact our support team first to resolve any billing issues.

11. Account Termination

If you receive a refund, your GrowwTv account will be terminated, and you will lose access to the service immediately. Any downloaded content will no longer be accessible.

12. Promotional Offers

Subscriptions purchased with promotional discounts or special offers may have different refund terms. Please refer to the specific terms of the promotion or contact customer support for details.

13. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Email: refunds@GrowwTv.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7
  • Address: 123 Streaming Street, Digital City, DC 12345
